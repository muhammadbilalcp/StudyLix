FAQ

Common questions

Is StudyLyx free?
Yes, completely free. Create an account and access all features — notes, flashcards, quizzes, video classes, and tools.
Which subjects are available?
Mathematics, Science, English, History, Geography, and Computer Science. Video lessons are currently available for Mathematics.
How does the daily quiz work?
Each day you get 10 questions from Maths, Science and English. Complete the quiz to earn XP, keep your streak alive, and unlock badges.
Is my data safe?
Your notes, flashcards, scores and progress are saved securely in Firebase Firestore with Google's enterprise-grade security.
