FAQ

Common questions

Is StudyLyx free to use?
Yes! StudyLyx is completely free. Create an account and access all features including notes, flashcards, quizzes, recorded classes, and study tools.
Which subjects are covered?
We cover Mathematics, Science, English, History, Geography, and Computer Science — with more subjects being added regularly.
How does the daily quiz work?
Every day you get 10 fresh questions from Maths, Science, and English. Complete the quiz to earn XP, maintain your streak, and unlock badges.
Is my data saved securely?
All your notes, flashcards, quiz scores, and progress are securely stored in Firebase Firestore with Google's enterprise-grade security.
Can I use StudyLyx on my phone?
Absolutely! StudyLyx is fully responsive and works beautifully on all devices — phones, tablets, and desktops.
